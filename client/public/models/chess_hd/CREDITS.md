# High-Quality Chess Piece Models

`chess_set_1k.glb` contains the 12 piece meshes (6 types × 2 colours) extracted
from the **Chess Set** asset by [Poly Haven](https://polyhaven.com/a/chess_set),
with the board and duplicate pieces removed and textures packed at 1K.
